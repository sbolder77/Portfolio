Contract

As a team we created a contract to agree on the approach, responsibilites and development we would use and develop through the module. The contract outlined the goals and objectives we would adhere to for the summative development assignment through the Secure Software Development module. The contract also covered expectations and policies and procedures we would follow to resolve potential conflict, disagreement or the need for support from the lecturer. It also outlined consequences around performance and disagreements. This encouraged a collaborative team effort and helped solidify the team work ethic we applied through the module.
